## 📊 **Project Overview**

This project successfully developed and deployed an AI-powered immigration content classification system that automatically identifies and categorizes immigration-related discussions from Reddit data. The system achieved exceptional performance with **96.9% accuracy** in classifying immigration content.

## 🎯 **Key Achievements**

### **Data Processing**
- **121,000 Reddit posts/comments** analyzed
- **14,976 immigration-related posts** identified (12.4% of total content)
- **298 data chunks** processed from AWS S3
- **100% data retention** rate with high-quality preprocessing

### **AI Model Performance**
- **Model Type**: BERT-based transformer classifier
- **Accuracy**: **96.9%**
- **Precision**: **93.9%**
- **Recall**: **96.9%**
- **F1-Score**: **95.4%**
- **Training Time**: 2 hours on 96,800 samples

### **Classification Categories**
1. **Green Card Discussions** - Specific green card process questions
2. **General Immigration** - Broader immigration topics and experiences  
3. **Irrelevant Content** - Non-immigration related posts
